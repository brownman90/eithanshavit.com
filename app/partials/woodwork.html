Lately I started doing some woodworking, something I wanted to do forever but never did. I was looking for classes around San Francisco but most of them did not fit my schedule. So I became a member of TechShop, a place where I can learn and experiment with many different tools. One very cool techonology they have is the ShopBot. It's a CNC router, which gives me the ability to use my graphic design skills and curve wood into complex shapes that would otherwise be very difficult to achieve. I'm still experimenting with it.

3 Light Bathroom Fixture

This is a single piece of wood cut using the ShopBot. To house the light bulbs I used parts of 3 simple ceramic bulb holders from the hardware store.
